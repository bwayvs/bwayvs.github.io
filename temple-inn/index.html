Reserve A Room

Here at Temple Inn & Suites, enjoy access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities for temple patrons who need to change to and from church clothes, a playroom and low-cost, short-term babysitting services, and long-term stay accommodations in kitchenette suites for full-time temple missionaries. Our temple history themed reception and sitting area provides a lovely environment for reflecting. We also have a wedding reception hall that can be easily customized with a simple catering menu. And we recently opened the new addition of a family search center that supports family search and ordinance work.
