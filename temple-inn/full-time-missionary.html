Full-Time Missionary

Request Details

Full-Time Missionary Couples working at the temple can find a home away from home here at Temple Inn & Suites. Whether you need a shuttle to and from the temple or want to work in Family History when not at the temple, we got you covered! We can accommodate various needs with Family ordinance card service and at-location Church Book Store. We have many quiet spaces throughout for study and reflection. Our temple-themed decor is sure to inspire reverence.

Extended-Stay Suites

Finding long-term accomodations while working at the temple can be challenging. That's why Temple Inn & Suites features a fully equipped kitchen in every room, complete with a stovetop, microwave and full-size refrigerator. A toaster, cooking utensils, pots and pans, dishes and silverware are available upon request.

Special Services for Full-Time Missionaries

  • Temple shuttle
  • Family ordinance card service
  • Family History Center
  • Family history consultation
  • Church Book Store
